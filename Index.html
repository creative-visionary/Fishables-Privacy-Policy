Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Information Collection

We do not actively collect or store any personal information from users. The extension operates entirely within your browser environment.

2. Authentication

If the extension requires authentication (e.g., signing in to access certain features), only the minimum necessary data for authentication may be temporarily stored — such as a token or user identifier — and it is used solely to maintain your session.

3. Data Usage

Any data that may be stored is used exclusively for providing the extension’s core functionality. We do not access, use, or analyze this data for any other purpose.

4. Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties under any circumstances.

5. Data Storage

Any information stored locally is only kept as long as necessary to provide the extension’s features. You may remove this data at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing local storage.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. If changes are made, the updated policy will be available on this page.
